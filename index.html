I just completed my PhD from the EECS department of Washington State University. My research focus is broadly on Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and currently I am advised by Dr. Jana Doppa.

More specifically, I am working on Anomaly Detection, Sequential Decision Making, Human-in-the-loop Learning, Sequence modeling using Deep Learning, Structured Prediction.

Before joining graduate school, I worked as Consultant, Professional Services at Escenic and Samsung R&D Bangladesh for more than four years. I have completed my bachelors in CSE from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.
