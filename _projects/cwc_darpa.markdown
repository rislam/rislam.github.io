[Communication With Computers](https://www.darpa.mil/program/communicating-with-computers) started from 2015. Our focus on this project is to build a symmetric semi automated system where human and machine can semlessly collaborate to solve a problem. We are working on the specific problem of **Blocks World** with a collaboration with UPenn, UIUC and UTD. The paper {% cite NarayanChen2017TowardsPS %} was published at RoboNLP workshop at ACL 2017 which contains the overall framework of the project.

Besides, we as a AI Planning team also published {% cite Das2017ActivePE %} at AAAI workshop on Human-Machine Collaborative Learning and recent AAMAS {% cite Das2018PE%} paper tried several approaches to solve the planning aspect of the project.
